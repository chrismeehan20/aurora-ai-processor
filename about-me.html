About the Developer

Meet Chris Meehan

I'm a teacher, edtech leader, and very amateur astronomer who loves building tools that make life easier.

With a passion for education and technology, I create applications like Aurora AI Processor for SheetsTM to enhance workflows and solve specific problems I encounter in my daily work.

Beyond Coding

When I'm not building apps or teaching, I love to travel. I've been fortunate enough to visit more than 40 countries, each offering unique perspectives and inspiration for my work and life.

On clear nights, you might find me stargazing with my telescope, attempting to photograph celestial objects, or simply enjoying the wonders of the night sky.

Philosophy

I believe in creating tools that serve a purpose rather than building technology for its own sake. The projects I develop are born from real needs I've identified in my own workflow.

My hope is that you find value in these tools as well. If something I've created helps make your work more efficient or solves a problem you've been facing, that's the greatest reward for me as a developer.
