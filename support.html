How to Use Our App

Installation

Visit the Google Workspace Marketplace and search for our app. Click "Install" and follow the prompts to grant necessary permissions. The app will be automatically added to your Google Workspace account.

Initial Setup

After installation, open Google Workspace and navigate to our app. Complete the initial configuration wizard by following these steps:

  1. Sign in with your Google account
  2. Select which Google Workspace services you want to integrate with
  3. Configure your preferences
  4. Save your settings

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install the app?

Visit the Google Workspace Marketplace, search for our app, and click "Install." Follow the prompts to complete installation.

What permissions does the app require?

Our app requires access to specific Google Workspace services to function properly. These permissions are requested during installation and are used only for the app's core functionality. For details, please review our Privacy Policy.

How do I update the app?

The app updates automatically through the Google Workspace Marketplace. You'll always have the latest version without any manual intervention.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We do not store your content on our servers longer than necessary to provide the service. For detailed information, please review our Privacy Policy.

How do I uninstall the app?

Go to Google Workspace Marketplace, find our app, and click "Uninstall." This will remove the app and revoke its access to your Google Workspace account.

Is there a cost to use the app?

Please check the app listing in the Google Workspace Marketplace for current pricing information. We offer different plans to meet various needs.

Does the app work on mobile devices?

Yes, our app is designed to work across all devices where Google Workspace is accessible, including smartphones and tablets.
